# Privacy Policy for Arborist Assistant

**Last Updated: October 28, 2025**

## Introduction

Arborist Assistant ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web service.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information
- **Account Information**: Email address, name, and password (encrypted)
- **Profile Data**: Professional credentials, company information (optional)

### 2. Location Data
- **GPS Coordinates**: Used for site creation and tree positioning
- **Address Information**: Site addresses for planning data lookups
- **Why We Collect**: Essential for mapping, site management, and regulatory lookups
- **User Control**: Location access can be disabled in device settings

### 3. User-Generated Content
- **Site Data**: Site names, addresses, notes
- **Tree Assessment Data**: Species, measurements, health assessments, photos
- **Voice Notes**: Audio recordings for site documentation
- **Photos**: Tree and site images
- **Drawings**: Custom site plans and annotations

### 4. Planning and Regulatory Data
- **VICMAP Data**: Automatically retrieved from Victorian Government APIs
- **Council Information**: LGA-specific tree laws and requirements
- **Overlay Data**: Planning overlay information

### 5. Technical Data
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, app version
- **Usage Data**: Feature usage, crashes, performance metrics
- **Firebase Analytics**: Anonymized usage statistics

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

1. **Core Functionality**
   - Creating and managing sites and tree inventories
   - Generating professional reports (PDF, Word, CSV)
   - Providing permit requirement lookups
   - Syncing data across your devices

2. **Service Improvement**
   - Analyzing app usage to improve features
   - Fixing bugs and technical issues
   - Developing new functionality

3. **Communication**
   - Sending service updates and announcements
   - Responding to support requests
   - Notifying about regulatory changes

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- **Device Storage**: All data stored locally using encrypted Hive database
- **Offline Access**: Full functionality without internet connection
- **Encryption**: Local data encrypted at rest

### Cloud Storage (Firebase)
- **Backup**: Optional cloud backup for multi-device sync
- **Encryption**: Data encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
- **Location**: Firebase servers (Google Cloud Platform)
- **Isolation**: Your data is separate from other users

### Security Measures
- Firebase Authentication with secure password hashing
- End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
- Regular security audits
- Secure API communications

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT sell your personal information.

We may share data only in these circumstances:

1. **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly authorize sharing
2. **Service Providers**: 
   - Firebase (Google) for cloud storage and authentication
   - Gemini AI for planning summaries (anonymized data only)
3. **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or legal process
4. **Business Transfer**: In case of merger or acquisition (you'll be notified)

### Third-Party Services

- **Firebase (Google)**: Authentication, cloud storage, analytics
  - Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
- **Google Gemini AI**: Planning data summarization (no personal data sent)
  - Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- **VICMAP API**: Victorian Government planning data (public information)
- **OpenStreetMap**: Map tiles (no tracking)

## Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Access**: Request a copy of your data
2. **Correction**: Update inaccurate information
3. **Deletion**: Request account and data deletion
4. **Export**: Download your data in portable format
5. **Opt-Out**: Disable cloud sync and use offline only

### Exercising Your Rights

Contact us at privacy@arboristassistant.com to:
- Request data deletion
- Export your data
- Update your information
- Ask questions about your privacy

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Backups**: Removed from backups within 90 days
- **Local Data**: You control local data deletion

## Children's Privacy

Arborist Assistant is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, contact us immediately.

## Location Data Specifics

**Why We Need Location:**
- Site positioning on maps
- Planning overlay lookups
- Permit requirement determination
- Tree geocoding

**Your Control:**
- Location permission can be revoked anytime
- Manual address entry available as alternative
- Location data stored only for your sites

**Not Used For:**
- Tracking your movements
- Advertising
- Selling to third parties

## Photos and Media

**Camera Access**: Used for tree and site photography
**Microphone Access**: Used for voice notes only
**Storage**: 
- Photos stored locally and optionally in Firebase Storage
- You control which photos to upload
- No facial recognition or photo analysis
