## üîç OVERLAY SUB-SCHEDULES

### VPO (Vegetation Protection Overlay)
- **VPO1, VPO2, VPO3...** - Each schedule has different requirements
- Common schedules:
  - VPO1: Native vegetation protection
  - VPO2: Significant trees
  - VPO3: Riparian vegetation
  - VPO4: Remnant vegetation
  - VPO5: Roadside vegetation

### HO (Heritage Overlay)
- **HO1, HO2, HO3...** - Each schedule protects specific heritage places
- Can include:
  - Individual heritage buildings
  - Heritage precincts
  - Significant trees (individually listed)
  - Historic gardens
  - Cultural landscapes

### ESO (Environmental Significance Overlay)
- **ESO1, ESO2, ESO3...** - Different environmental values
- Common schedules:
  - ESO1: Waterway protection
  - ESO2: Habitat protection
  - ESO3: Wetland protection
  - ESO4: Biodiversity corridors
  - ESO5: Endangered species habitat

### SLO (Significant Landscape Overlay)
- **SLO1, SLO2, SLO3...** - Different landscape areas
- Common schedules:
  - SLO1: Ridgeline protection
  - SLO2: Coastal views
  - SLO3: Scenic routes
  - SLO4: Rural landscape
  - SLO5: Urban landscape character

### BMO (Bushfire Management Overlay)
- **BMO, BMO1, BMO2...** - Different bushfire risk areas
- Schedules may vary:
  - Defendable space requirements
  - BAL (Bushfire Attack Level) zones
  - Exemptions for vegetation removal

### DDO (Design and Development Overlay)
- **DDO1, DDO2, DDO3...** - Hundreds of schedules
- Each schedule has specific requirements:
  - Building height limits
  - Setback requirements
  - Landscape requirements
  - Tree retention requirements
  - Canopy cover targets
