Privacy Policy for Arborist Assistant

Last Updated: October 28, 2025

Introduction

Arborist Assistant ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web service.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and password (encrypted)
  • Profile Data: Professional credentials, company information (optional)

2. Location Data

  • GPS Coordinates: Used for site creation and tree positioning
  • Address Information: Site addresses for planning data lookups
  • Why We Collect: Essential for mapping, site management, and regulatory lookups
  • User Control: Location access can be disabled in device settings

3. User-Generated Content

  • Site Data: Site names, addresses, notes
  • Tree Assessment Data: Species, measurements, health assessments, photos
  • Voice Notes: Audio recordings for site documentation
  • Photos: Tree and site images
  • Drawings: Custom site plans and annotations

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

  1. Core Functionality - Creating and managing sites and tree inventories
  2. Service Improvement - Analyzing app usage to improve features
  3. Communication - Sending service updates and responding to support

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Device Storage: All data stored locally using encrypted Hive database
  • Offline Access: Full functionality without internet connection
  • Encryption: Local data encrypted at rest

Cloud Storage (Firebase)

  • Backup: Optional cloud backup for multi-device sync
  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Location: Firebase servers (Google Cloud Platform)

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT sell your personal information.

We may share data only in these circumstances:

  1. With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing
  2. Service Providers: Firebase (Google), Gemini AI (anonymized only)
  3. Legal Requirements: If required by law or legal process

Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request account and data deletion
  • Export: Download your data in portable format
  • Opt-Out: Disable cloud sync and use offline only

Exercising Your Rights

Contact us at hello@arboristsbynature.com.au to:

  • Request data deletion
  • Export your data
  • Update your information
  • Ask questions about your privacy

Location Data Specifics

Why We Need Location:

  • Site positioning on maps
  • Planning overlay lookups
  • Permit requirement determination

Your Control:

  • Location permission can be revoked anytime
  • Manual address entry available as alternative
  • Location data stored only for your sites

Not Used For:

  • Tracking your movements
  • Advertising
  • Selling to third parties

Children's Privacy

Arborist Assistant is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
