Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first site?

Click the "+ Add Site" button on the dashboard, enter the address, and tap "Load Permits" to retrieve planning data.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! All data is stored locally on your device. You can work completely offline, and changes will sync when you reconnect.

How do I export my data?

Go to the Site Map tab and choose from PDF, Word, or CSV export options. Each format serves different purposes.

Where is my data stored?

Data is stored locally on your device and optionally backed up to Firebase cloud storage if you enable sync.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted both locally and in transit. Your data is private and never shared with third parties.
